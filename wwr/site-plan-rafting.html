Audience

Who are the target customers?

The target customers for a website catering to people who love rafting are likely to be adventure enthusiasts, outdoor lovers, and individuals who enjoy water sports. They may vary in age but generally fall within the range of 18 to 50 years old. Financially, they may have disposable income for recreational activities and travel.

What do they want?

Customers accessing the website are likely seeking information and resources related to rafting. This could include details about popular rafting destinations, safety guidelines, equipment recommendations, and perhaps organized rafting trips or events. They may also be interested in connecting with like-minded individuals or communities who share their love for rafting.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

The audience is likely to access the website through various devices, including desktops, laptops, and mobile devices. Since outdoor enthusiasts are often on the go, having a mobile-friendly platform is crucial to cater to their needs while they plan their rafting adventures.
